Blockchain Smart Contracts Development Services

Blockchain-powered smart contracts are self-executing digital protocols that regulate agreements between participants in a blockchain network. They are non-interchangeable, making them a powerful tool for various business applications. The primary purposes of implementing smart contracts are automating the processes and eliminating the need for mediators. Our blockchain development team implements smart contracts using Ethereum, Hyperledger, and EOS to solve multiple industry problems.

Minor and major vulnerabilities in the smart contracts are capable of creating failures in the main network, which would eventually result in the loss of millions of dollars. This creates the need for better security practices and regular audits.

At EXODIA, we provide state-of-the-art smart contracts audit development services for smart contracts. Our expert team of smart contract auditors can perform audits for tokens, stablecoins, crowdsales, NFTs, marketplaces, DeFi, and more. We have a suite of static & dynamic analyses, automated tools, and a double-stage review system to ensure 100% authenticity of your project.

Benefits Of Smart Contracts For Businesses

Automation

Smart contracts are self-executing protocols, which, once written, are automatically triggered. They process and execute transactions at an incredibly high speed.

Independent

Smart contracts operate independently once deployed. They eliminate the need for manual initialization or intermediary participation, thereby reducing the costs.

Transparency

Smart contracts are visible to all participants. This way, they offer increased transparency to all, and no parties can create a dispute once the contract is deployed.

Trust

Smart contracts eliminate the room for errors, thereby rendering the agreements transparent, autonomous, & secure and increasing the trust among participants.

Accuracy

Smart contracts are faster and cheaper to execute. They lower the possibility of manual errors since they are automated, and the data is stored in different locations.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are smart contracts?

    With the advent of blockchain technology, the use of smart contracts increased significantly. Blockchain-powered smart contracts are self-executing computer protocols. They regulate the agreements between traders and are recorded on a blockchain. Smart contracts control the overall transaction execution and also make them transparent, traceable, and irreversible. The primary purposes of implementing smart contracts are automating the processes and eliminating the need for mediators - central authority, third-party agencies, or brokers - from the transaction processes.

  • What are the advantages of smart contracts?

    • Smart Contracts eliminate the need for intermediaries, which in turn reduces manual errors.
    • Blockchain-powered smart contracts provide increased transparency to all the network participants, and no parties can create a dispute once the contract is set.
    • Smart contracts process and execute transactions at an incredible speed, thereby helping businesses save more time and money.
    • Smart contracts are designed with the highest levels of security.
    • Smart contracts eliminate the room for errors, thereby rendering the agreements transparent, autonomous, and secure.

  • What types of audit services do EXODIA provide?

    EXODIA provides various smart contract audit services like preliminary audit, security audit, and full chain audit.

  • Where are smart contracts used?

    Smart contracts are widely used in financial institutions, digital identity, healthcare sector, educational organizations, logistics & shipping, and escrow services.

  • What is smart contract audit?

    Smart contract audit is the cross-analysis of the existing smart contract codes on a blockchain. It is performed by audit experts to identify potential bugs and security vulnerabilities in the code.
