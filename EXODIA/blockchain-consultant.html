Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does a blockchain consultant do?

    The blockchain consulting process at EXODIA is as follows:

    • Project Ideation
    • Assessment & Feasibility Check
    • Workflow Outline & Documentation

    Once we mutually agree on the project, we move on to the development stage.

  • How can I schedule an appointment with the EXODIA team?

    • Go to the Schedule An Appointment section.
    • Select the duration of the meeting you prefer.
    • Choose the time & date comfortable for you.
    • Provide your Name, Email, Phone number, and the Budget you would like to spend on the project.
    • Choose the person you would like to schedule the meeting with.
    • If you have any questions or want to tell us more about the project, write it down in the message box, and click Schedule Event.
    • You will receive an email with the confirmation status and the link to join the meeting.

  • What is BaaS?

    Blockchain as a Service. It is the process through which a blockchain company provides enterprise-grade blockchain development services for industries or businesses.

  • How experienced is the consulting team?

    Our consulting team is a mix of blockchain professionals, technical experts, marketing managers, and business analysts. They have the best industry knowledge that comes in handy when you need the best services to incorporate blockchain into your business.

  • Is it possible to create a separate blockchain for my business?

    Yes, it is possible. Since you want to develop a separate blockchain for your business, it is best to go with the private blockchain. It helps to limit access to concerned parties and store confidential business records securely.

  • What is the cost of setting up a private blockchain?

    The actual cost to set up a private blockchain will vary based on the business requirements, blockchain type, and tools to be used.
