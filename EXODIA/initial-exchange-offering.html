Why IEO For Fundraising?

IEOs are considered the most easiest and secure means to fundraise for a project. Given the benefits they offer, individuals and businesses prefer IEO over other crowdfunding methods. In EXODIA’s opinion, here are the six reasons to choose IEO for a blockchain or crypto project.

Trust

An IEO project undergoes screening and background checks before being listed on an exchange, which makes the project assertive and creates a state of trust among investors.

Transparent

The project details like roadmap, scope, modules, and team members are literally open to view by investors, which adds more trust and makes the project more transparent.

Secure

Investors need not worry about the concerns related to their investments in an exchange-backed IEO project since the exchange double checks the project’s & team’s credibility.

Exchange Listing

Unlike other crowdfunding methods, IEOs are listed only on crypto exchanges. The exchanges take the responsibility of validating the projects and the investors.

Quick Process

Compared to other fundraising methods, IEO processes are quite simple and quick. Since all the verifications are pre-checked, investments begin as soon as the project is listed.

High Success Rate

IEOs have been considered the most successful platforms to raise funds since they’re available globally. Investors from anywhere in the world can participate in an IEO.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Initial Exchange Offering?

    IEO, also known as Initial Exchange Offering, is a potential fundraising program. An entry-level company or an individual launch an IEO with the aid of an IEO development company and raise funds in exchange for their ideas. The investors who are interested in the project invest in the offering with digital currencies and they receive tokens in favor of their support. The exchange platform provider also promotes the project on their own platform for the existing investors with different IEO marketing services such as push notifications, banners, and ads until the project becomes a real-time application.

  • What is the future of IEO?

    We provide the best crypto bot development services to develop an efficient, lucrative, and secure bot embedded with trending features. We also provide a platform where you can develop a bot with your own strategies. Moreover, IEOs are now evolved to become IDO (Initial Dex Offering), IGO (Initial Game Offering), and ILO (Initial Liquidity Offering).

  • Is it difficult to launch an IEO?

    No. With the right solutions, it’s never a difficult task to launch an IEO for your project. At EXODIA, we provide you with end-to-end services to develop tokens for your IEO, launch in an exchange, and promote it on a global scale.

  • What happens after IEO?

    Once all investigations have been completed, and the IEO project has passed muster, the platform posts a notice of token sale allowing investors to purchase these utility tokens. The company issuing the IEO will typically also list the tokens on their own site to publicize their availability.
